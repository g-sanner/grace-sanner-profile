machine language.

### Background

I grew up in a small town in Northern Illinois, with a big family of 3 brothers and 2 sisters. During
high school, I took an AP Computer Science course with one other student. The typical response to that 
was that "girls don't last in the technology world". I took that as a personal challenge.

I transferred to NIU after receiving my Associate's of Science from Rock Valley Community College,
where I graduated with Honors.

Over the course of the last two summers, I have interned at the software company, Ticomix, where I 
was working with the .NET framework to create ERP websites for a variety of clients.

Now a senior, I hope to expand my experience with the technology world and remind everyone that 
you belong. We all do.
